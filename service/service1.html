Blackhawks Exhibitions

Blackhawks Exhibitions recognizes that a perfect exhibition booth design represents the brand image and standards while demonstrating the marketing pitch. We consider a couple of factors when creating exhibition booth design ideas for our clients:

Target Audience:

Are you outreaching to individuals or businesses (B2B)? And what is the job or role of the majority of customers to that particular exhibition? Is it primarily decision-makers or influencers?

Goals for Exhibition:

What do you expect to gain from this exhibition? How many potential leads are you looking for? Do you want to talk to the new prospects or existing clients? What does it take for you to be satisfied with the outcome of your exhibition participation?

Examine and Sense:

What is your preference for exhibition stand design? A contemporary or innovative look? Maybe something a little more refined? Is your design required to be product- or image-oriented? What about the values and guidelines of your brand?

Communication:

What message do you want your Booth to convey? What do you want to leave an impression on the visitor's mind?

Budget:

We provide booths at reasonable and competitive prices. Whatever your financial situation, we have a solution for you.
